Hi, my name is **Junle Chen**. My website is available at [https://junle.site](https://junle.site).

This site is my personal research workspace. I use it to keep long-form notes, quick memos, daily arXiv paper reading, Zotero paper lists, and links related to my research workflow.

The current focus of the workspace is agent planning, agentic RL, multi-turn interaction, long-horizon planning, and automation tools that help maintain a reliable paper-reading workflow.

The site includes:

- **Notes**: Markdown notes opened inside the site reader, with MathJax, outline, and comments.
- **Memos**: quick timeline-style records with GitHub owner login and Supabase realtime sync.
- **Academic**: Daily Paper and Zotero Paper List views for paper triage and reading.
- **Realtime state**: shared memo, paper star, and archive status powered by Supabase.

Feel free to explore the site and reach out if you find anything interesting.
