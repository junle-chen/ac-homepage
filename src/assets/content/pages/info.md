# Junle Chen(陈俊乐)

First-year PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Professor Xiaofang Zhou.

## About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. My current research focuses on large language models, long-horizon agents, and spatial-temporal data.

## Education

- **Sept 2021 - Jun 2025**: Bachelor of Computer Science and Technology, School of Future Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology.
- **Jul 2023**: AI Cloud-Based Scientific Research with Data-Driven Knowledge, LAMDA, Nanjing University.
- **Sept 2025 -**: PhD in Computer Science and Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Under the guidance of Professor Xiaofang Zhou.

## Research Interest

- Large Language Models
- Long-horizon Agents
- Spatial-temporal Data
